**EVENING**

On the road, dust is resting.

On the river, mist presses.

That  is  not

Lonely weeping,

Corncrake in the marsh

Creaking.

On the bench a near star shimmers, A cigarette in the darkness glimmers.

From afar,

Unexpected,

Footsteps are,

By the fence there.

¬´To  the kitchen garden, Michal dear¬ª.

¬´Why?¬ª

¬´For an onion¬ª.

¬´To kiss you

And embracing

At the gate there,

They walk beneath the watchful moon

In the dew,

For berries seeking,

Where is corncrake's

Sad creaking.
